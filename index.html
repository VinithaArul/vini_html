About Us

Vicky Construction is an Coimbatore, India owned company that has built an honorable reputation based on quality and excellent work ethics. Vicky Construction has been helping Victorians in constructing their Great Australian Dream! Enjoy the highly competitive rates with a personal service. Since launching, Vicky Construction has since developed successfully in the construction sector providing developments not only on time but in many cases before time! With over 15 years experience in the building industry and Vicky Construction has become a master builder. We do not emphasize on production line manufacturing of houses but specialize in tailoring products that are suitable to the client in design and budget.
